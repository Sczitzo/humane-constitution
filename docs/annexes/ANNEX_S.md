> **Provenance:** Foundational constitutional annex · Hardens the Constitutional Review Panel against capture and failure · Status: **ACTIVE**
**Purpose.** The CRP is a small but load-bearing institution. This Annex hardens it against capture and defines what happens if the CRP itself becomes compromised.

### S1. Composition hardening
- The CRP shall include a primary panel and a reserve pool selected through staggered sortition from an eligibility pool screened for conflicts, competency thresholds, and recent political or financial entanglement.
- Terms must overlap and rotate so no single founding wave or transient coalition can dominate the whole panel.
- No member may serve consecutive terms beyond the published limit, and cooling-off periods apply before service in regulated institutions or elected office.

### S2. Classification and downgrade safeguards
- Any downward classification of a proposed rule change - for example, treating a Tier 2 or Tier 1 issue as Tier 3 - requires a supermajority of the full sitting panel.
- If the panel is materially split on classification, the rule auto-escalates upward pending external review rather than drifting downward by default.
- Minority opinions must be published whenever classification is disputed, and dissent automatically extends the public challenge window.

### S3. Capture indicators and forced review
- Repeated pro-incumbent outlier rulings, undisclosed contact patterns, synchronized financial conflicts, or abnormal dissent suppression trigger mandatory capture review.
- A capture review may be triggered by the Ombuds Office, an oversight supermajority, a defined public petition threshold, or an internal CRP minority bloc.
- During capture review, the CRP may not approve any downward classification or emergency constitutional suspension unless an external mixed panel concurs.
- Capture-indicator monitoring of panelists' contact patterns and dissent behavior is conducted under privacy minimization: only the minimum signal needed to detect coordination or conflict is collected, access is logged and time-bound, and raw material is purged once a review concludes. This monitoring exists solely to detect capture and may never be used as a loyalty test, a tool of dissent suppression, or a means of profiling lawful disagreement. Dissent itself is never an adverse indicator; the watch against capture must not become an instrument of capture.

### S4. Constitutional failure recovery ladder
- **Stage 1 - Freeze:** contested reclassifications and emergency exception expansions pause automatically.
- **Stage 2 - Substitute review:** a temporary mixed review body drawn from reserve members, regional courts, and citizen-auditor slots hears only the disputed class of cases. The substitute body is subject to the same conflict-screening, recusal, and disclosure regime as §S1 panel members; members who would fail §S1 screening are ineligible to sit.
- **Stage 3 - Reconstitution:** if capture is confirmed, the CRP is partially or fully reconstituted under emergency anti-capture rules and public scrutiny. The emergency anti-capture rules carry an automatic sunset and expire by default; they may extend only by affirmative re-ratification on the record, so that emergency reconstitution cannot ossify into permanent altered governance.
- **Stage 4 - Refounding trigger:** the recovery window is bounded and published in advance, not open-ended. If constitutional review is not restored within that window, the Annex H refounding back-stop is triggered, and that trigger runs through the Federated Ombuds and a lot-drawn citizen panel rather than through the captured CRP, so refounding never depends on the body it is meant to replace.

### S5. Confirmation of capture and termination of the regress
- Capture is confirmed not by the panel under suspicion but by an authority structurally distinct from it: the Federated Ombuds together with a lot-drawn citizen panel, with the rotating external chamber concurring where one is seated. Confirmation requires clear and convincing evidence drawn from the §S3 indicators and the substitute-review record.
- The same structurally distinct authority screens the Stage-2 substitute body for conflicts before it sits, applying the §S1 regime.
- This ladder does not pretend to an infinitely regressing guarantor. Where the chain of review cannot be fully closed, it terminates honestly at full publication of the record and lot-drawn citizen oversight — never at an unaccountable final office. The last word rests with an open record and ordinary people drawn by lot, not with a sealed authority above challenge.
