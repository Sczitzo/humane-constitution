# ANNEX O. Reader Scenarios and Authority Map
**Purpose.** The protocol should not require new readers to reverse-engineer the system from abstractions alone. These scenarios make the architecture concrete and show which annexes govern difficult edge cases.

### O1. Small manufacturer building a new workshop
- The enterprise receives Flow, not Essential Access, for payroll and materials. Ordinary operating float is exempt from idle decay up to published levels; larger long-horizon build funds move into a project escrow under Annex J.
- The project escrow is milestone-audited, time-bound, and non-convertible into civic weight or essential entitlements. If milestones stall or funds are diverted, special treatment ends.
- **Relevant authorities:** Article V, Annex J, Annex G4, and Annex H if a rule change is attempted.

### O2. Caregiver with a six-month family crisis
- The caregiver retains survival-floor protections through Essential Access continuity and may seek a Protected Pause Window under Annex K so prior Voice and Service Record are not wiped out during the crisis. Verified hardship preserves proportionate civic continuity; anti-concentration decay for high balances applies to ordinary inactivity, not documented incapacity or caregiving emergency.
- Contribution earned through caregiving and mutual aid remains eligible for multi-path verification rather than being erased because it occurred outside a formal payroll context.
- **Relevant authorities:** Articles IV and VI, Annex K, Annex L for appeal, and Annex H if any attempt is made to narrow the hardship pathway.

### O3. Region claims housing scarcity
- The region must publish evidence class, capacity definition, challenge path, and unwind condition under Annex M. Counting uninhabitable or unstaffed units as placeable capacity is prohibited.
- Affected persons may challenge the declaration on expedited review; false or discriminatory scarcity is a constitutional violation.
- **Relevant authorities:** Articles III, V, and VII, Annexes I and M, and Annex L for review.
