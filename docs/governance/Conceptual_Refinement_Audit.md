# Conceptual Refinement Audit

**Role of this document.** This is the macro/micro critique layer for the Humane Constitution. It does not replace the Constitution, Threat Register, Threat Resolution Matrix, Open Problems Resolution Docket, Architecture Source Map, External Evidence Register, Patch Log, Claims and Evidence Register, or Pilot Evidence Roadmap. It explains where the whole design is strongest, where it is still brittle, and which concepts need sharper tests before the project can honestly claim more than serious design readiness.

**Core standard.** The project should become harder to fool, not easier to sell.

---

## 1. Macro Thesis Under Review

The central theory is instrument separation:

1. **Survival** is protected through Essential Access and the Constitutional Survival Minimum.
2. **Markets** continue through Flow, enterprise, contracts, and bounded investment.
3. **Public power** is separated from wealth through Voice, Service Record limits, non-convertibility, and review bodies.
4. **Scarcity** is handled through reality-anchored measurement and temporary Shared Storehouse rationing.
5. **Legitimacy** depends on local consent, exit, public records, and a patch process that can repair itself.

The theory is coherent at the level of purpose. Its hard problem is not whether the values fit together; they mostly do. The hard problem is whether the institutions needed to protect those values create a second bureaucracy powerful enough to recreate the domination the system is trying to prevent.

## 1A. First-Principles Collapse States

The [Architecture Source Map](./Architecture_Source_Map.md) states the project as a defense against three collapse states. These are not political labels. They are system failures.

| Collapse state | First-principles failure | Project response | Main evidence burden |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Survival-Trade Bind** | A person must succeed in markets, employment, credit, or exchange to stay alive or civically legible. | Essential Access, CSM, identity-continuity rules, capacity measurement, and non-convertibility. | Identity recovery, Essential Access delivery, shadow-convertibility tests, appeal continuity, and scarcity drills. |
| **Power-Wealth Convergence** | Wealth becomes formal or practical authority over rules, enforcement, measurement, or institutional design. | Flow / Voice / Service Record separation, public records, beneficial-control review, anti-rent controls, and independent oversight. | Proxy-market tests, anti-corruption audit, public-banking simulation, legal-wrapper review, and FAP capture testing. |
| **Static-Advantage Loop** | Temporary advantage hardens into permanent class position. | Demurrage, decay, rotation, anti-dynasty rules, new-participant access, subsidiarity review, exit protections, and bottleneck audits. | Service Record misuse testing, concentration dashboards, legal-wrapper review, founding-legitimacy tests, and governance red-team exercises. |

The strongest version of the thesis is not "separate the instruments and trust the wall." The stronger version is: **every advantage that can affect another person's survival, rule-entry, or opportunity must remain contestable, visible, appealable, and unable to become a universal converter.**

## 2. Highest-Risk Unresolved Concepts

| Rank | Concept | Why it is load-bearing | Current posture | What refinement must do |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Identity without surveillance or exclusion | Essential Access and civic integrity both depend on knowing continuity of personhood. | Active-unproven design, unresolved evidence problem. | Run the existing identity evidence gates: false exclusion, recovery delay, data exposure, staff discretion, coercion, abandonment, no-token access, and vulnerable-population failure. |
| 2 | Real-capacity measurement | Essential Access and Shared Storehouse fail if numbers drift from physical reality. | Strong architecture, little field evidence. | Test oracle latency, disagreement, standards capture, confidence bands, and physical sampling cost. |
| 3 | Contribution without coercive hierarchy | Markets and dignity need real contribution, but Service Record can become rank. | Designed controls, still culturally fragile. | Prove that incentives reward work without becoming a worth score or insider credential. |
| 4 | Anti-rent enforcement against legal wrappers | The economic theory fails if dynastic control persists through trusts, shells, foundations, or control rights. | Designed direction, legal implementation gap. | Run jurisdiction-specific trust, beneficial ownership, land, and enterprise-control review. |
| 5 | Founding legitimacy and consent | A non-coercive constitution cannot begin through coercive rollout. | Strong written Founding Order, no real coalition test. | Define who may found, who may object, how consent is measured, and how exit is tested before dependency forms. |
| 6 | Bureaucratic complexity and institutional self-protection | The safeguards themselves can become an expert class. | Threats named, many controls proposed. | Reduce status inflation, test ordinary-reader comprehension, and red-team review-body self-protection. |

## 3. Macro Contradictions to Keep Testing

These are not fatal contradictions yet. They are pressure points that must stay visible.

| Tension | Project promise | Counter-pressure | Required discipline |
|---|---|---|---|
| Survival is unconditional, but identity must be checked. | No one loses the survival floor because of status or compliance. | Fraud controls can become the real survival gate. | Identity disputes must preserve the CSM floor while recovery is reviewed. |
| Markets remain free, but survival goods leave ordinary price allocation. | Enterprise and trade continue. | Producers may redirect effort toward non-essential profit unless procurement and capacity rules work. | Pilot supply response and vendor concentration before claiming market compatibility. |
| Voice is civic, not money. | Public influence cannot be bought. | Service Record can become informal class status. | Ban social ranking by effect, not just by label; test employer/vendor misuse. |
| Measurement anchors reality. | Issuance and scarcity track physical capacity. | Measurement institutions can become the new sovereign. | Require visible uncertainty, independent methods, and public explanation of disagreement. |
| Local consent prevents coercion. | Units can join, leave, and keep local decisions local. | Essential floor and shared infrastructure require federation-level guarantees. | Keep the dignity floor universal while forcing every other escalation through subsidiarity review. |
| Patch process makes the system repairable. | Threats can be corrected without collapse. | Patch governance becomes a gatekeeping priesthood. | Status language must distinguish proposed design, active control, and evidence-backed resolution. |

## 4. Micro Refinement Rules

Use these rules when editing threats, patches, annexes, and public claims:

- **Do not say "closed" when the control is only designed.** Use "proposed", "designed", or "awaiting activation" unless the control is ACTIVE and integrated.
- **Do not say "resolved" without evidence.** Resolution requires test results, not a plausible mechanism.
- **Every load-bearing solution needs a failure test.** If a clause protects a right, name the way it could fail and the evidence that would expose failure.
- **Separate moral clarity from operational certainty.** "Survival should not depend on income" is a moral claim; "this delivery mechanism works" is an evidence claim.
- **Treat bureaucracy as an adversary.** Every review body, panel, registry, dashboard, and oracle is also a capture surface.
- **Prefer fewer stronger queues.** A long list of "closed" items is less honest than a short list of unresolved preconditions.

## 6. What Not to Do

- Do not add more institutions merely because a risk exists.
- Do not let a dashboard substitute for power limits.
- Do not convert every unresolved question into a patch unless the patch contains an enforceable mechanism and a test path.
- Do not claim public readiness because the document corpus is internally cross-linked.
- Do not let the app or presentation layer become evidence that the constitutional design is operationally proven.

## 7. Success Standard for the Next Revision

After the next revision, a skeptical reader should be able to say:

1. I understand the core theory.
2. I can see the strongest unresolved risks.
3. I know which mechanisms are only designed.
4. I know what evidence would strengthen or falsify each major claim.
5. I can tell where to pressure the project next.

For the core Commons Return and Universal Stake claim, the immediate pressure point is now the [Commons Return and Universal Stake Evidence Test Package](./Commons_Return_Universal_Stake_Evidence_Test_Package.md), which defines the fiscal adequacy, incidence, public-finance, procurement, legal-wrapper, privacy, and legitimacy tests required before stronger claims are allowed.
