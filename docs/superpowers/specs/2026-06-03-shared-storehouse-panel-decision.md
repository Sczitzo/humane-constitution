# Shared Storehouse — Panel Decision Record

**Status:** Proposed — design decision, not incorporated. Annex AQ, Annex M, and the constitution are unchanged. Outcome of an adversarial panel run under the [Instrument Evaluation Rubric](2026-06-03-instrument-evaluation-rubric.md).

**Problem:** allocate a genuinely scarce essential FAIRLY under verified shortage — not by price/wealth — TEMPORARILY, never cutting below the inviolable survival floor (Essential Access), and never hardening into a permanent emergency power.

---

## Result in one line

The deadliest failure in rationing is not unfairness — it's the **emergency-power grab** (Rome's dictatorship → Caesar; the Reichstag Fire Decree that never lapsed; UK bread rationing running to 1954, nine years past the war). So the panel made the **anti-emergency-power, anti-wealth-buyout scaffold the spine**: a capacity-indexed, automatic, sunset-bound, floor-protecting formula with **no human who decides who gets cut** — and plugged the other mechanisms in as the *allocation rule*.

Rejected as primary: **tradable coupons** (breaks the wall by construction — lets the poor sell survival and the rich rebuild price-allocation) and **queue/time-rationing** (excludes the time-poor/disabled, pure deadweight loss, proxy line-standers re-import wealth).

## Decision: the chassis + plug-in architecture

**The chassis — a capacity-indexed automatic formula:**
- A multi-source oracle measures *supply-side capacity* (stock/flow), never persons. When capacity falls below trigger, allocations adjust by a published formula — **no authority decides who is cut.**
- The survival floor is a hard constant the cut mathematically cannot reach (the formula operates only on headroom *above* Essential Access).
- **Hard sunset + re-verification clock + automatic supply-recovery off-switch** — the anti-permanent-emergency device. History's lesson: rationing's fatal flaw is not unfairness, it's *not ending*.
- **Asymmetric tier lock:** the formula *curve* is Tier-3 tunable (fast, corrigible); the *trigger threshold* and *protected minimum* are Tier-1/2 (slow, adversarial-panel, timelocked). You can fix the curve fast; you cannot quietly move the floor or the trip-wire.
- **Oracle hardening:** quorum of independent measurements, fail-to-continuity on divergence, published provenance. *Confident measurement error*, not oracle silence, is the deadliest threat (same finding as Essential Access).

**The allocation rule that plugs into the chassis:**
- **Divisible goods (food, water, fuel): need-weighted equal shares** — an equal per-capita base (maximally legible, legitimate, dignity-preserving; refuses to rank human worth) PLUS a capped need-supplement tranche above the floor for verified categories (medical need, pregnancy, children, heavy laborers — the UK ration-book tier model). **Not** pure-proportional (need-blind cuts hurt the high-need) and **not** pure-equal (unjust when needs differ).
- **Indivisible goods (one treatment, one bed): triage into coarse, pre-committed, auditable need-bands → lottery within band.** Triage supplies need-matching; lottery is the capture-proof tiebreaker among roughly-equal claims (transparent commit-reveal RNG + public roster + non-transferable, single-use, expiring entitlements + guaranteed fallback to the divisible floor for losers).

**Hard constraints baked in:**
- Allocations are **non-transferable and non-saleable** (holds the wall). Tradability is permitted only *above* the survival floor for non-survival surplus — never for the floor itself.
- **Need-tiers are attribute-attested at the registry/credential layer** ("qualifies for tier 2"), never via standing in-crisis assessment or consumption surveillance — this resolves triage's panopticon failure.
- Pre-published priority/need ordering is a **Tier-2 amendment** (adversarial panel, sunset), never in-crisis discretion — this resolves triage's Babel/weaponization failure.

## How it locks onto Essential Access

The Essential Access decision explicitly hands *gap-rationing under scarcity* to Shared Storehouse — and this chassis IS that mechanism. They share: the capacity oracle (measure capacity, never persons), the asymmetric override (oracle may only tighten toward the formula; Tier-1 holds the floor and trigger), and "confident error is the threat." The two instruments are one coherent survival-under-scarcity subsystem.

## Validates the corpus direction, with three corrections

The corpus's apparent direction (capacity-indexed automatic, Annex AQ) is **largely vindicated** as the right scaffold. Corrections:
1. **Replace the need-blind proportional default with a need-weighted formula** (need-tiers above the floor) — equal-percentage cuts are catastrophic for the high-need.
2. **Add triage→lottery for indivisible goods** — a proportional formula can't ration lumpy goods (one ventilator).
3. **Attribute-attest need-tiers at the registry layer, not in-crisis** — keep the anti-panopticon property.
