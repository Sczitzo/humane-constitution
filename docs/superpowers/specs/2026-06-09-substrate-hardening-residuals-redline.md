# Substrate Hardening — Residuals Redline (panel-revised)

**Status:** Proposed redline — NOT incorporated. Awaits explicit human approval + the applicable amendment process. The constitution and annexes are unchanged. Discharges the residuals of the capstone's three remaining substrate fixes, re-scoped against current live text (2026-06-09) and **revised after a three-member panel** (adversarial, Christ-centered, corpus-fit).

**Panel headline.** My first-draft self-assessment ("two narrow residuals, one open item") understated the risk. **Fix 2 was the most dangerous of the whole arc** — an external re-founding trigger as drafted was a *cheaper coup tool* than the capture it defends against (one capturable body, no threshold, immediate, repeatable) — and is heavily revised below. Fix 1's "narrow" residual carried a live INV-001 conflict (the conservative cap would under-issue below the floor in thin-capacity regions). Fix 3's survival-decoupling was *false* for above-floor survival-critical needs (a chronic medication above the CSM baseline). All three are revised. Corpus-fit also caught a bad cross-reference: I cited INV-020, which does not exist on main (it is only a *proposed* number in the scope redline) — corrected.

**Grounding finding (unchanged).** Main's June-7 hardening already closed most of the oracle fix (ANNEX_AL Directional-Bias Failure Criterion + the over-reporting tripwire) and most of the founding fix (ANNEX_AH §AH5.1 people-triggered re-founding + honest circularity acknowledgment). The genuine open work is the identity-recovery mechanism.

## Fix 1 — Oracle over-reporting (narrow residual, INV-001-subordinated)

**Already done (no change).** ANNEX_AL's Directional-Bias Failure Criterion + *"If overstatement bias would permit issuance above verified physical capacity, the lower physical-sampling or conservative estimate governs immediately"* + adversarial-seat certification + the 14-day suspension chain. The over-reporting tripwire is bound.

**Residual + redline — amend INV-005 Mechanical boundary, subordinated to INV-001:**

> *(replace)* The capacity figure must be confirmed by **at least two independent physical-sampling (Tier-3) oracle nodes of distinct methodology class (ANNEX_AL)**, no two sharing an operator, data vendor, or governing body. **Where two independent samplers cannot be seated for a category (capacity-thin or disaster-affected regions), this requirement may never reduce Essential Access below CSM where any real capacity exists:** issuance proceeds on the single best physical estimate distributed by the INV-001 manna rule, and the shortfall is handled as an honest System-Failure declaration (ANNEX_Y §Y4) with named accountability (INV-011) — not a quiet conservative-cap under-issue. The two-sampler requirement governs *above-floor* issuance integrity; it never starves the floor in the regions least able to seat two nodes.

**Companion (reserve drawdown — bound against fraud).** The survival floor may be drawn from committed strategic reserves (ANNEX_AT) **without a current oracle reading** when directional bias is flagged or the oracle is suspended — but only: authorized by the named INV-011 accountable human **with Ombuds co-sign**, on a **dual flag** (the bias/suspension flag *and* an independent reserve-custodian confirmation), **rate-limited to the CSM floor amount** (never above-floor), and logged to the Article VII dashboard within 24 hours. A bias flag alone may not open the reserve.

**Tier.** INV-005 amendment Tier-1; reserve clause Tier-2.

## Fix 2 — Founding re-ratification (heavily revised: Nathan, not a coup)

**Already done.** ANNEX_AH §AH5.1 people-triggered re-founding petition (500/5%), Dignity-Only Continuity Mode, the 180-day trigger, the CSM continuity guarantee, and the honest AH6 circularity acknowledgment.

**The danger in the first draft.** An external body that could *open the re-founding window directly* on its own finding — no petition threshold, immediate effect, repeatable every 90 days — moves the attack surface from "capture 7-of-9 keyholders" to "capture *one* attestor," and lets a *false* finding topple a *legitimate* founding (a false witness, Exod 20:16). That is a worse failure mode than the slow circular audit it replaces.

**Revised redline — add to ANNEX_AH §AH5.1, keeping the watchman, barring the false accuser:**

> **External-attestation re-founding trigger (constrained).** An independent attestation body — conflict-screened on the ANNEX_N pluralism standard, **not appointed by the keyholders it may find against** — may, on a published capture finding against the keyholder set, **lower the AH5.1 petition threshold to its opening level (500 / 5%)**; it may *not* open the re-founding window unilaterally. Humans still petition. The finding has force only when: (a) it rests on **published evidence meeting the stated capture-finding standard**; (b) it is **signed by named persons bearing attestation-at-risk stake (ANNEX_AS)** — a false finding is itself a staked, auditable act; (c) it is **corroborated by a second, independently-appointed attestor** with no shared appointer (mirroring the two-key Tier-1 architecture, P-034/ANNEX_AV); and (d) the keyholders found against receive a **published rebuttal window and independent adjudication of the finding before the petition outcome binds** — never delaying the survival floor (INV-001 continuity is unaffected throughout). **Anti-churn:** a finding rebutted on adjudication bars the same attestor from re-triggering for a published cooldown, and repeated false findings are an INV-006 auditable capture event against the attestor itself. This adds an exogenous breaker the captured keyholders do not control, without becoming a single capturable trigger or a false-witness coup tool; it does not claim to dissolve the bootstrapping circularity (acknowledged in AH6).

**Tier.** Tier-1 (founding/amendment machinery; relates to INV-007). Note: it opens a *petition window*, not a Tier-1 parameter change — any resulting re-founding still runs the full FAP; P-014 stays permanently closed.

## Fix 3 — Identity recovery (the open work; four cases the panel surfaced)

**The gap.** A rich recovery *test* package exists; the bound *mechanism* does not. A lost/frozen identity strips Voice, Service Record eligibility, the above-floor top-up, and net-worth/membership standing at once.

**Redline — a bound Identity Recovery Protocol (new governance instrument, patch P-073 — see sequencing note), designed to pass the test package as a *blocking* gate:**

1. **Survival decoupled from recovery (Tier-1, already INV-001).** The CSM floor is presence-triggered and never waits on identity recovery; recovery restores only above-floor standing.
2. **Above-floor survival-critical sub-tier (panel-added — the sharpest fix).** Survival-critical needs *above* the CSM baseline — a chronic medication, a disability accommodation, an established care arrangement — get **provisional continuation on the emergency (4-hour) track without full identity recovery**, treated like the floor, not like Voice. "Survival never waits on identity" must cover survival-critical-above-baseline, not only the basket.
3. **k-of-n social attestation; no single point** — never dependent on a single device, biometric, document, or authority.
4. **Dominated-social-graph protection (panel-added).** Because k-of-n is a quorum, not a coercion test (k coerced attestors look like k honest ones), any valid k **must include at least one cross-regional/institutional attestor independent of the person's local social graph**, and recovery **completes only after a coercion-detection interview** (test §5), with **fast reversal** when coercion is found.
5. **Safety-shielded — never routes through the controller.** Recovery may never require contacting an abuser, employer, landlord, sponsor, or hostile institution; safe alternate contact, protected interview, independent advocate, and emergency manual access are guaranteed.
6. **Isolated-person binding path (panel-added).** For a person with no safe attestor (trafficked, the abused whose whole graph is the controller, the stateless refugee), a **guaranteed, time-bound, advocate-led affirmative path** — a single independent advocate plus the cross-regional pool — reconstitutes above-floor standing **within the test-package time ceilings without any locally-sourced attestor**; its outcomes are **counted in the exclusion denominator** so the gap is visible. Recovery must be good news to the captive and the stranger (Lev 19:34; Luke 4:18), not only to the well-connected.
7. **Capture-resistant attestation** — cross-regional/independent pools + attestation-at-risk (ANNEX_AS) to deter false attestation.
8. **Offline roster** — manual fallback so a frozen digital registry cannot empty the recovery path.
9. **Data-minimizing + compartmentalized roster (panel-added).** Recovery uses attribute-proof/selective disclosure; and the attestor roster itself — which is relationship data — is **compartmentalized/encrypted so no operator can read the social graph absent an active recovery event**, subject to the test §4 linkage red-team as a blocking pass condition. Harmful flags are corrected post-recovery.
10. **Guardianship preserves standing** — supported decision-making preferred; delegation never becomes ownership; the dependent's own standing is preserved (ties to INV-001 and the Children & Dependents doctrine on the design branch; *note: the interface invariant numbered INV-020 in the scope redline is only proposed, not yet live — this protocol does not depend on it*).

**Tier + process.** New governance instrument + patch **P-073** *(sequencing: depends on P-071/P-072 from the prior redlines shipping first; otherwise renumber to the next free id)*. Survival-decoupling + the above-floor sub-tier are Tier-1-grounded (INV-001); the recovery mechanism is a Tier-2 founding commitment, **gated on passing the Identity Recovery Evidence Test Package as a blocking gate** — Stage 3 Recovery Stress Drill + Stage 4 Privacy & Coercion Red Team, including the explicit "every pre-set attestor is the abuser" dominated-graph case.

## Christ-centered review (panel-incorporated)

- **Alignment.** Fix 1 is two or three witnesses (Deut 19:15; Matt 18:16) + Joseph's storehouse (Gen 41) — no floor resting on one corruptible eye, grain held against the lean years. Fix 2 is Nathan to David (2 Sam 12) *with* a guard against the false witness (Exod 20:16): a staked, corroborated, contestable finding. Fix 3 is the Good Shepherd seeking the lost sheep (Luke 15:4–7) without marching it back through the wolf, and now reaching the trapped and the stateless (Lev 19:34; Luke 4:18), not only the well-connected.
- **Babel-risk.** All three reduce it. The residual was Fix 2's external trigger becoming a rival tower; the two-attestor + staked + contestable + anti-churn revisions close that.
- **Human dignity.** Fix 3's compartmentalized roster + attribute-proof refuse to reduce the abused/displaced to an exposed dossier; the above-floor sub-tier refuses to strand the person whose survival is partly above the basket.
- **Fruit test.** Good fruit if Fix 3's recovery gate stays *blocking* (the spec holds that line) and the isolated-person path is bound (it now is). Bad-fruit tripwires — a recovery route that leaks to a coercer, a captured attestation pool, a false capture finding toppling a legitimate founding — are each met by a named guard the panel required.
