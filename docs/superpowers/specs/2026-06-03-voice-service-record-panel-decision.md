# Voice + Service Record — Panel Decision Record

**Status:** Proposed — design decision, not incorporated. Annex Z and the constitution are unchanged. Outcome of an adversarial panel run under the [Instrument Evaluation Rubric](2026-06-03-instrument-evaluation-rubric.md).

**Problem:** the civic/power instruments — Voice (how civic influence/agenda/priority works) and Service Record (who is eligible to hold public roles) — must prevent Power-Wealth Convergence (wealth/intensity/faction capturing civic power) and the Static-Advantage Loop (an entrenched civic elite), while holding the **Annex Z firewall**: a contribution/eligibility record may gate eligibility to serve but must never convert into civic voice, market money (Flow), or survival preference (Essential Access); voice must never be buyable.

---

## Result in one line

The **firewall + non-accumulation principle is vindicated as the spine** — it is exactly why liquid democracy and reputation/meritocracy fail (they are built from the accumulation/transfer/conversion the firewall forbids), and it is the incumbent's genuine strength. But the panel (one member read the actual Annex Z) surfaced **three real corrections**, and elevated **sortition** to a co-primary role the corpus likely underweights.

## Decision: the layered civic architecture

**VOICE (civic influence) — layered:**
- **Everyday agenda/priority = bounded non-accumulative civic priority** (the incumbent principle: equal, expiring, non-transferable, non-stackable claim to elevate an issue). This **beats quadratic voting** (collusion-proof + legible) and **liquid democracy** (no concentration) on the exact axis the constitution cares about. QV's own advocate conceded it is "a simpler, collusion-proof version of what I'm trying to do" — you give up QV's convex intensity-pricing to buy collusion-resistance and legibility, which for *this* constitution is the right trade.
- **Episodic high-stakes deliberation = sortition / citizens' assemblies** — allotted mini-publics set agenda and draft on big questions; the best capture-resistant deliberative engine on the panel (Irish assemblies, Ostbelgien, the French climate convention). Residual capture migrates to *agenda/information control* — guard that.
- **Consent / ratification / recall = equal franchise** — one-person-one-vote for binary legitimacy gates (ratify what assemblies propose; recall office-holders). Unbeatable on legitimacy; use it as the consent layer, not the steering wheel.

**SERVICE RECORD (eligibility for public roles) — layered:**
- **Selection = sortition from the eligible pool** (allotted, rotating office — capture-resistant). The pool may be record-**gated** but never record-**weighted** (equal-probability lot, or the firewall breaks).
- **For narrow technical roles = competence as a binary pass/fail license** — the one legitimate kernel salvaged from meritocracy: a *lock, not a ladder*. Role-scoped, expiring, firewalled from influence/status/wealth/survival, rotation-paired. The moment it becomes a score that ranks and travels across domains, it is the dystopia again.

## Three corrections to the corpus (the panel read Annex Z)

1. **Voice is currently minted from contribution (Annex Z2), not flat-equal.** That is a material equality wound *and* a surveillance driver (you can't mint from "verified contribution" without a contribution ledger — failing G-3). **Fix: decouple Voice from contribution-minting → a flat, equal, expiring claim.** Contribution belongs only to Service Record eligibility, behind the firewall.
2. **The firewall is legal, not physical.** Annex Z4 bans fused scores, but T-009's documented "Network Pause Ring" (8-person rings holding eligibility indefinitely) already breaches it. **Fix: harden the wall with active composite-score / ring detection and enforcement — a legal ban it cannot self-execute is not a wall.**
3. **Voice may be theater.** An expiring claim to a *hearing* that decides nothing won't be used (Family 7). **Fix: give Voice teeth — a bounded *binding* trigger** (e.g., guaranteed ballot placement / mandatory agenda slot), not just a guaranteed audience.

## What's vindicated

Non-accumulation + rotation + the firewall genuinely outperform QV, liquid democracy, and reputation systems on the named failure modes. The incumbent's core bet is right. It is simply **not strong enough as written** without the three fixes — and the corpus underweights **sortition**, which the panel makes co-primary for both deliberation (Voice) and office selection (Service Record).
