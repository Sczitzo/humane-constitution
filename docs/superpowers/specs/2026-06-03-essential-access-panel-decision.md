# Essential Access — Panel Decision Record

**Status:** Proposed — design decision, not incorporated. Annex Y, Annex M, Annex AB, and the constitution are unchanged. Outcome of an adversarial panel run under the [Instrument Evaluation Rubric](2026-06-03-instrument-evaluation-rubric.md).

**Problem:** the Survival-Trade Bind — survival (food, shelter, medicine, water) must NOT depend on market position. Essential Access is the unbuyable floor, walled off from market money (Flow) and meant to be non-convertible, and it must hold under scarcity.

---

## Result in one line

No single delivery mechanism clears the gates. The panel converged on a **layered floor built on one principle: guarantee the *good* (provision side), not a policed *token* (surveillance side)** — and the floor is an **axiom/right, not a measurement or an application.** The two designs closest to the corpus's current direction (earmarked vouchers, capacity-measured issuance) were both demoted with specific corrections.

## Decision: the layered Essential Access floor

1. **The floor is an unconditional, presence-triggered RIGHT to necessities** — asserted as an axiom, not measured, not means-tested, not applied-for. This is what earns G-3 (no panopticon), G-1 (inclusion by default), and G-5 (fail-safe). A floor you must *claim* or *qualify for* leaks exactly at the most vulnerable (take-up failure, identity gaps, biometric denial).
2. **Delivered as universal in-kind / decommodified provision** for the goods where non-convertibility is *intrinsic* and choice matters least — healthcare, water, children's nutrition, emergency shelter. (You can't cash a hospital bed; the wall is structural, not policed.) Provided through a **plural set of providers + a public option — never a monopoly** (monopoly provision fails G-4 Babel and G-6 weaponizable).
3. **A universal public provider of last resort underneath** = the catastrophe **fail-safe** and supply-of-last-resort, so the right is always redeemable even when markets collapse and cash/tokens are worthless. **Universal access, never residual** (the residual "poor services for poor people" trap drives stigma, low take-up, underfunding, and political death — Korpi-Palme).
4. **Capacity measurement as infrastructure underneath, not as the floor-issuer** — measure supply-side **capacity, never persons**; capacity governs only **gap-rationing under verified scarcity** (handing to Shared Storehouse), and via **asymmetric override** the oracle may only *raise* issuance — humans hold any cut. Doubles as the ecological-limits sensor.
5. **A bounded cash top-up ABOVE the wall (Flow-side)** for autonomy and flourishing — explicitly **not** part of the survival floor. Cash is the right instrument for flourishing above survival and the wrong one for survival itself, because survival must not be convertible and cash *is* convertibility.

## Design principles that emerged (transferable)

- **Guarantee the good, not a policed token** — get non-convertibility from the provision side, so survival never rides on surveilling what the poor buy.
- **The floor is an axiom/right, not a measurement or a claim.**
- **Universal, never means-tested or residual.**
- **Plurality, not monopoly provision** (fixes Babel + weaponizable).
- **Measure capacity, never persons; capacity rations the gap, never gates the right.**
- **Cash lives above the wall (flourishing), never below it (survival).**

## Corrections to the corpus's apparent current design

- The corpus leans on **non-convertible earmarked claims** (closed-loop credit, Pilot A Cohort 2). Correction: **don't achieve non-convertibility by policing purchases** — that is a panopticon (G-3) and conditions trivially in emergencies (G-6). Get non-convertibility from provision instead; use earmarked vouchers only as a choice-preserving top-up *behind* a guaranteed floor.
- The corpus leans on **capacity-measured issuance** (Annex Y/M). Correction: **don't let measurement GATE the right.** Assert the floor as axiom; measure capacity not persons; asymmetric override (oracle raises, humans cut); treat *confident measurement error*, not oracle silence, as the primary threat.
