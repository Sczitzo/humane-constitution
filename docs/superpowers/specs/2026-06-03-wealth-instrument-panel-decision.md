# Wealth Instrument — Panel Decision Record

**Status:** Proposed — design decision, not incorporated. Annex D and the constitution are unchanged. This records the outcome of an adversarial panel run under the [Instrument Evaluation Rubric](2026-06-03-instrument-evaluation-rubric.md).

**Problem:** Power-Wealth Convergence + the Static-Advantage Loop — prevent wealth from converting into durable political power, and inherited advantage from hardening into caste, without making survival depend on the market.

**Incumbent under test:** progressive net-worth demurrage (Annex D), 26–46%/yr on assessed net worth above a participation floor, single instrument.

---

## Result in one line

The confiscatory single-instrument net-worth demurrage **fails a gate** (G-3, no panopticon — it must continuously surveil all of a person's assets to function) and is **replaced** as the primary lever by a calibrated hybrid. Every panelist, including demurrage's own advocate, voted to demote it.

## Gate outcomes

| Mechanism | Gate result |
|---|---|
| Net-worth demurrage (26–46%) | ❌ Fails **G-3 (panopticon)** — continuous valuation of all assets = surveillance state |
| Social wealth fund (active) | ❌ Fails **G-4 (Babel)** — an active megafund is a single dominating capital allocator; survives only passive + ring-fenced |
| Harberger/COST (universal) | ❌ Fails dignity gate — universal forced-sale treats homes/heirlooms as fungible; survives only as a bounded valuation module |
| Received-inheritance tax | ✅ Clears all gates |
| Predistribution + capital endowment | ✅ Clears all gates |
| Land-value tax | ✅ Cleanest gate sheet |

## Scored survivors (rubric families, 1–5; floor = 2)

LVT and the social-wealth-fund/endowment cluster lead; no survivor falls below floor, so each is viable as a *component* — none is adequate alone, which is the point. Notably the ecology family (10) independently rewards LVT (discourages land-banking/sprawl) and exposes that inheritance and endowment do nothing ecological.

## Decision: adopt the hybrid

Replace the single confiscatory demurrage with a portfolio across *different bases*, each covering another's gated or floored weakness:

| Component | Role | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Land-value tax** | Foundation / revenue engine | Flight-proof (land can't move), near-zero deadweight, already-assessable, best on ecology. Misses non-land wealth. |
| **Progressive received-inheritance tax** | Anti-dynasty precision | Taxes the actual transmission event of the static-advantage loop; reaches equity/financial wealth LVT misses. Blind to the living oligarch. |
| **Universal capital endowment** | Dispersal engine | Attacks the loop at the starting line; funded by the two above. Best distributive justice. Needs the tax legs. |
| **Social wealth fund + citizen's dividend** | Ownership equalizer + distribution channel | Funded from LVT, kept **passive and ring-fenced** (or it hits the Babel gate). Dividend routes into Flow above the Essential Access wall. |
| **Harberger/COST** | Bounded valuation *module* | Best answer to "what is this illiquid asset worth?" — invoked only to price illiquid assets, with statutory carve-outs for home/heirloom/Essential Access. Not a spine. |
| **Net-worth tax, 1–2%** | Continuous backstop | Reaches living-generation equity/financial fortunes the others miss between transfers — *only* viable paired with self-assessment to escape its own panopticon gate. Confiscatory rates are dead. |

## Key implications

- **Breaks Annex D §D9 (Tier-1 single-instrument invariant).** The hybrid is a stack. Defensible because the instruments hit *different bases* (land rent ≠ transfers ≠ equity ≠ distribution) — a portfolio, not double-taxation on one base, which is the spirit §D9 guarded. But adopting it requires a Tier-1 amendment and a rewrite of Annex D from "the carrying-cost instrument" to "one of several calibrated levers." Cascade: Annex D, INVARIANTS, SPECIFICATIONS, the FC-05x reconciliation, and Pilot B's framing.
- **Simplicity cost.** The single instrument was legible and had one capture surface; the hybrid is more robust but harder to explain and has more exemption surfaces. A real trade (rubric tension: legibility ↔ robustness).
- **Pilot-gated.** Tier-2 components require pilot evidence before activation (ties to Pilot B). Scores are directional; pilot data overrides argument.
