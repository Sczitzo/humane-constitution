## Fix 1 — The Productivity Register (one canonical "productive" determination, settle-forward)

**The problem (two layers).** "Productive" unlocks Flow issuance (ANNEX_X) *and* the Commons Return exemption (ANNEX_D §D3) on separate tests, so the same activity can be classified "productive" by the looser test to claim both. The panel found the deeper flaw is **temporal**: Flow mints at commitment *start*; the Commons Return exemption is assessed at *settlement*. A holder takes an honest "productive" determination at T0, mints Flow, then exits productive use before any reversal clears — the Flow is already created and moved. Separate criteria do not stop a timing attack.

**The fix — a shared Productive-Status Register, settle-forward, with an appeal-bound determiner.** New governance instrument (`Productive_Status_Register`), patch **P-072** (see numbering note), anchored in ANNEX_J's published Article V stewardship standard and consumed by ANNEX_X and ANNEX_D:

1. **One determination, recorded once, three roles separated.** Each commitment/asset is assessed for productive status once, against the single published Article V stewardship standard. The **standard-author** (Article V / ANNEX_J), the **register-adjudicator**, and either benefit's **issuer** must be three structurally separate roles (INV-006; reinforced by Fix 2) — written in, not left to inference.
2. **Settle-forward escrow (closes the temporal double-dip).** On commitment-linked assets, the Commons Return §D3 exemption is **not pre-granted**; it is escrowed and vests only after the Flow-side commitment is verified to have *stayed* in productive use through one full settlement period. If the commitment fails or exits productive use first (the ANNEX_X "enhanced audit when issuance exits productive use too quickly" trigger), the escrowed exemption never vests and the deferred public return is collected — while wages already paid stay protected by the no-clawback rule. Reversal is replaced by non-vesting, so the gaming cannot pay off before it clears.
3. **Each benefit checks its own criterion** against the shared record; the looser test for one benefit may never be borrowed for the other. A Commons Return finding of passive extraction (ANNEX_J) refers any reliant Flow issuance to the ANNEX_X membrane/asset-equivalence review.
4. **Payroll/escrow fast-path (removes the bottleneck).** Time-critical issuance below the ANNEX_X de-minimis threshold proceeds on a *provisional* productive status; the register catches up and settle-forward (clause 2) governs the exemption. Issuance is never blocked waiting on an adjudication; the double-dip stays closed because the exemption is escrowed regardless.
5. **The determiner is humble and correctable.** A productive-status determination is appealable through the ANNEX_J independent-review path; the determiner publishes its basis and the criterion applied, bears the bounded burden of proof (a holder is not presumed an extractor; an honest steward is not put to impossible proof), and is itself audited by a structurally independent party (INV-006, "no apex may sit unaudited"). It applies the published standard; it does not author the boundary while deciding a case.
6. **Status classifies the activity, never the person.** Productive status is a classification of an asset/commitment, never a standing judgment of a person's worth (INV-003), and stays within the §D6 minimum-necessary-data rule (no new surveillance surface).

**Tier + process.** Tier-2 founding commitment; new governance instrument + patch **P-072** *(sequencing: P-071 is claimed by the Commons Return Should-Fixes redline; P-072 is correct only if that ships first — if this redline is processed standalone, renumber to P-071)*. Evidence-test entry: does settle-forward close the temporal double-dip without starving legitimate issuance; is the determiner non-capturable given its appeal + audit + Fix-2 recusal.

## Fix 2 — Generalize recusal to economic-parameter-setting (INV-006 extension)

**Already present.** INV-006 (structural separation, not disclosure; no apex unaudited) + the annex recusal web. **Residual:** no Tier-1 rule stops an *economic-parameter-setter* from setting a parameter that moves their own holdings — the Service-Record→office→source-base/oracle-district/rationing-formula/floor-S→own-bracket→re-mint-standing loop.

**The fix — extend INV-006, with a materiality line and a sortition-reconciliation clause (panel-required):**

> *(amend INV-006 Statement — append)* This separation extends beyond verification to **governance and economic-parameter-setting**: no person or body that sets, votes on, drafts, or adjudicates an economic parameter of any instrument may be positioned to benefit *disproportionately* from the distributional effect of that parameter.
>
> *(amend INV-006 Mechanical boundary — append)* No person or body may set, vote on, draft, or adjudicate an economic parameter — Commons Return source-base definitions and rates, oracle district boundaries, the Shared Storehouse rationing formula, Flow issuance parameters, or the household savings floor S — where the parameter's published distributional effect on their own holdings, source-base classification, oracle district, or a materially-beneficial entity is **disproportionate and individuated**: that is, materially different from the effect on the median participant, above the ANNEX_J de-minimis ordinary-household floor. A *universal* effect (e.g., a change to S that moves every below-floor household alike) does **not** trigger recusal; a change that *uniquely* exempts or advantages the setter's own asset class, district, or entity does. The general-law-versus-self-dealing line is the test.
> - **Structural separation, not disclosure**, for the body that sets economic parameters relative to the pool that benefits disproportionately.
> - **Named materiality judge:** whether an interest is disproportionate and individuated is determined by an independent methodology-class body (ANNEX_AL-style; ANNEX_D §D6.4) and **published before the vote**, so recusal cannot be invoked selectively, late, or by manufactured interest to remove one reformer while similarly-situated members remain.
> - **Recusal never removes standing.** Recusal removes a person from *setting* the parameter; it never removes their one-person-one-vote standing on it (INV-001, INV-004).
> - **Sortition reconciliation:** a sortition body does not collapse on universal-effect parameters. Members with a disproportionate individuated conflict recuse and are backfilled from the draw; if no conflict-free quorum can be seated, the parameter escalates to the one-person-one-vote legitimacy gate below rather than being decided by a rump.

**The one-person-one-vote legitimacy gate (a distinct mechanism — flagged by corpus-fit).** Separately from the recusal rule, a *material* change to a wealth-adjacent economic parameter requires a one-person-one-vote legitimacy gate before taking effect. This is **not** merely a generalization of INV-006; it adds a procedural precondition on top of the existing FAP/Tier processes (Tier-3 standard tuning, Tier-2 supermajority+panel). It must carry its own tier-interaction analysis at intake — specifically, which "wealth-adjacent" parameters it gates and how it composes with Tier-3 standard-FAP tuning — and should be proposed as a *named companion mechanism*, not folded into the INV-006 extension.

**Tier + process.** The INV-006 extension is Tier-1 — 7-of-9 keyholders + 180-day timelock + `modifies_tier1` two-key adversarial attestation (ANNEX_AV) + Christ-centered review. The 1p1v companion gate carries its own tier-interaction analysis.

## Christ-centered review (panel-incorporated)

- **Alignment.** Fix 1 is honest weights and measures — one true standard for "productive," not a loose ruler for the powerful (Prov 11:1; Deut 25:13–16) — now with the determiner made humble and correctable (Prov 11:14; the ANNEX_J appeal path). Fix 2 is authority as service, not self-dealing (Mark 10:42–45; the faithful steward, Luke 16:10–12), with the materiality line ensuring it disciplines the rich man's self-dealing without silencing the poor reformer (Matt 18; Matt 7:12).
- **Babel-risk.** Both reduce it. Fix 1's residual (a single determiner as little tyrant) is bounded by the three-way role split + appeal + audit. Fix 2's residual (recusal as a weapon to disenfranchise honest small-holders) is bounded by the de-minimis floor, the "never removes 1p1v standing" rule, and the published-before-the-vote materiality judge.
- **Human dignity.** Neither touches households; both point at rule-makers and asset classifications. Productive status classifies the activity, never the person (INV-003).
- **Fruit test.** Good fruit: one honest definition of productive that the powerful cannot game twice; rule-makers who cannot feather their own nest; the double-dip closed without clawing back honest wages; the small honest holder kept in governance. Bad-fruit tripwires (a classification bottleneck; an emptied governance pool) are closed by the fast-path and the sortition-backfill clause.
